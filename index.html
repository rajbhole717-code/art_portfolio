About Me

The Artist Behind
the Strokes

I'm Raj — a self-taught pencil and charcoal artist driven by a passion for capturing the essence of the world around me. From the subtle curves of a human face to the grandeur of sweeping landscapes, every sketch is a meditation on light, shadow, and emotion.

My work spans portraits, landscapes, mythological subjects, wildlife, and anime fan art — each piece reflecting my belief that the simplest tools can create the most powerful art. Every line is placed with intention, every shadow crafted to tell a story.
